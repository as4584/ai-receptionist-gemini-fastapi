# Onboarding Checklist

A quick list of what we need to configure your AI Receptionist and go live smoothly.

## Business & Contact
- Legal business name and primary contact (name, email, phone)
- Physical time zone and languages supported
- Emergency/after-hours contact and escalation Slack channel (optional)

## Hours & Policies
- Business hours (weekday/weekend/holiday); lunch or blackout windows
- Cancellation/no-show policy (how you handle fees and rebooking)
- Average ticket size and priority services (for routing/triage)

## Services & Routing
- Services offered (durations, resources, buffers)
- Routing preferences (book vs. escalate; when to collect payment)
- FAQ topics to answer via AI vs. human

## Telephony
- Current provider and primary phone numbers
- Desired live setup: call forwarding vs. new number
- SMS handling preferences (auto-replies, opt-in language)

## Calendar & Systems
- Calendar platform (Google/Microsoft/other) and scoped credentials
- Booking rules (lead time, double-booking, reschedules)
- CRM or POS integrations (optional)

## Payments (optional)
- Payment processor (Stripe/Square/other) and when to request payment
- Refund/chargeback guidance

## Feature Flags
- Enable RAG answers, allow AI booking, require human review for edge cases
- Any compliance constraints (PII redaction, data retention)

## Final Checks
- Test tenant set up and dry-run call flow approved
- Escalation playbook verified (who, when, how)
- Go-live date/time scheduled and monitoring enabled
